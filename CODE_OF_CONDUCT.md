# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

HandUMI contributors and maintainers are expected to provide a welcoming,
harassment-free environment. Be respectful, accept constructive feedback,
credit others' work, protect participant and operator privacy, and prioritize
safe laboratory practice. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing,
publication of private data, and unsafe pressure to bypass hardware or human
subjects procedures are unacceptable.

## Scope and enforcement

These standards apply in repository spaces and when representing the project.
Maintainers may edit or remove content and temporarily or permanently restrict
participation when conduct threatens people, privacy, the project, or its
community.

Do not put sensitive conduct reports in a public issue. Until the project owner
publishes a dedicated private conduct contact, use GitHub's private platform
reporting tools for urgent abuse and contact a repository owner through a
previously established private channel. Establishing and approving a durable
project-private conduct contact remains a release-governance requirement.

Retaliation against a good-faith reporter is prohibited. Reports should be
handled confidentially to the extent reasonably possible, with conflicts of
interest disclosed and recused.
